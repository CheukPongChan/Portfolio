Overview

Throughout my Mphil studies and full-time Research Assistant career, I had taken part in major teaching duties in 6 different University courses or workshops for over 3 years. My responsibilities as a course tutor ranged from preparing tutorial notes prior to start of semester, to solving technical questions faced by students at any given time during the tutoring session. I mainly educate students on how to create VR applications via Unity and our in-house VR plugin -- VotanicXR.

In my opinion, the most rewarding factor as a University tutor, is how I can utilize VR experiences to facilitate teaching by creating immersive VR applications and tools for students to interact with. This way pupils can be actively involved in the course content and gain a concrete understanding of it through straight-forward visualization.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developing a VR visualization tool for students to preview a task virtually before implementing it in real life
  • Developing a Multiplayer Archaeology VR visit application for students to explore huge dig sites with real-time remote virtual guidance by the professor
  • Preparing tutorial notes and sample VR scenes from scratch to aid students' learning
  • Organizing students in groups and expediting discussions between groupmates
  • Assessing students' learning capabilities and adjusting teaching speed accordingly
  • Frequently solving any technical difficulties faced by students swiftly enough to avoid interrupting the lesson
  • Reviewing and grading assignments and exams
  • Organizing workshops for students in any Engineering department to experience VR game creation
