Overview

In The Soul Vanquisher, you play as a member from the government special task force trying to investigate a massive missing persons case. Not long after, you awake in an unknown underground dungeon and have to uncover the secrets behind it in order to find an exit to the hellhole. The dungeon is filled with items and collectibles for the player to scavenge, and there are moving traps to prevent any escaping. Not only that, A mysterious figure lurks around the dungeon, and anyone who is brave enough to confront it without the right gear, will face its wrath.

The game starts off as an "Escape Room" experience, where the player is carefully trying to gather resources and intel about the dungeon. As the game goes on, the player gains strength through hints from diary pages and stronger weapons, and is able to face off against the creature at the end.

Key Features

  • The game is readily playable on the web via WebGL support
  • A wide arsenal of weapons, where some of them can be strengthened by combining them with another weapon
  • A diary with flippable pages, providing important lore and hints to players
  • An intricate level design to ensure that each distinct area in the game has an impact to game flow as well as the lore behind it
  • Full pathfinding support for the antagonist via NavMeshAgent
  • Player controls completely transform towards the end game
