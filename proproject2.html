Overview

As a Mphil student and Full-time research assistant, one of my responsibilities is to mentor multiple student summer interns each year. Usually, individual interns work on their own Unity VR applications, and the themes of these applications were mostly related to Engineering.

But in Summer 2022, we invited a few interns from different Engineering departments to work on a casual game as a group project. Since every intern has little to none experience in game development and OOP principles, my goal was to lead them to complete their game from initial storyboarding, to every subcomponents across the application. I also introduced OOP concepts to them so that they can grasp the general rules of game programming.

Key Responsibilities

  • Expediting brainstorming sessions to discuss storyboard, gameplay features and game flow
  • Arranging regular meetups to follow up on scheduling and workload distribution
  • Transferring essential game development skills, including C# programming, OOP principles eg.Encapsulation, suitable design patterns, time management, prototyping, and game design principles eg.Risk and reward
  • Manage and assist in the implementation of every subcomponents, such as VR hardware device tracking, AI animations, VFX, UI, and Audio system
  • Organizing occasional game sessions to relieve stress and strengthen team bond
